About Thinodiki Thinantha

At Thinodiki Thinantha, we specialize in what our customers love most – Biryani! Our menu is a celebration of rich tradition and irresistible flavors. Whether you crave a spicy, aromatic Non-Veg Biryani or the subtle, soul-satisfying taste of our Veg Biryani, every dish is made with passion and authenticity. We use only fresh ingredients, home-style techniques, and secret spice blends passed down through generations. Come experience the most-loved biryanis in town – where every bite feels like home!
